## technical_support

**Triggers:** not working, problem, bug, frozen, black screen, slow, won't turn on.

**Steps:**
1. Ask at most 2 targeted diagnostic questions (power, last action, indicator lights).
2. Propose a solution from FAQ or general troubleshooting.
3. If unresolved after 2 attempts, create a `ticket` and offer human handoff.

**Exit:** user confirms fix, or ticket created → `scenario_state: null`.
